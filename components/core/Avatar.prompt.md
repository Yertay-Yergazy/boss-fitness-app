Circular avatar for users and coaches. Falls back to gold initials on dark background when no image is provided.

```jsx
<Avatar src="/assets/coach-1.png" name="Дмитрий Волков" size="lg" />
<Avatar name="Анастасия Ким" size="md" border="strong" />
<Avatar size="xl" border="none" />
```

**Sizes:** xs(28), sm(36), md(48), lg(72), xl(96), 2xl(120)  
**Borders:** gold (default), strong (solid gold), white, none
