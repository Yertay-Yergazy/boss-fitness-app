Pill-shaped status label for subscription states and general tagging. Always uppercase, always on a dark surface.

```jsx
<Badge status="active">АКТИВЕН</Badge>
<Badge status="expiring">ИСТЕКАЕТ СКОРО</Badge>
<Badge status="expired" dot={false}>ПРОСРОЧЕН</Badge>
<Badge status="inactive" size="sm">Неактивно</Badge>
```

**Statuses:** active (gold), expiring (amber), expired (red), inactive (gray), success (green), info (blue)  
**Sizes:** sm, md, lg
